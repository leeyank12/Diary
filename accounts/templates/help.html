Welcome to the DiaryGo Help Page. Here you can find answers to common questions about ordering, delivery, milk quality checks, and our policies.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

You can browse products on the Home page and click "Add" to add items to your cart. Go to the Cart page to complete the order.

What time do you deliver milk?

Milk is delivered every morning between 5:00 AM and 8:00 AM.

Can I cancel an order?

Yes. Orders can be cancelled before the daily cutoff time (9 PM). Refunds or adjustments will be applied automatically.

Milk Quality Tests We Perform

To ensure safety and purity, every batch of milk undergoes:

  • Lactometer test (checks density & dilution)
  • Foam/Shake test (detects detergent or impurities)
  • Smell and freshness test
  • Cream/fat layer check
