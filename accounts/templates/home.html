Our Farm Process

Our delivery agents follow a strict, step-by-step process to ensure that every drop of milk collected from farmers is pure, fresh, and safe for customers. The process begins with the collection of milk directly from trusted farmers. Once collected, the first quality check is performed using a lactometer, which helps measure the density of the milk and detect dilution. After this, a quick on-spot purity test is conducted by shaking the milk in a clean bottle‚Äîpure milk produces thin foam, fewer bubbles, and does not emit any unpleasant odor . Only when the milk passes all checks is it accepted. The delivery agent then logs the collected quantity and farmer details into the website system for transparency and accurate tracking. After verification, the milk is made available for customer orders and delivered fresh to their doorstep.
